About

Welcome to my introduction site!

My name is Zsolt Orbán and I am 34 years old. Three years ago I have decided to learn IT. Due to the lack of time I kept delaying my plan. Then came the first lockdown, and I dived right into the book called 'Coding for Dummies' by Nikhil Abraham. Luckily, I also have two good friends (and mentors) who happen to be working in IT for over 10 years and they also helped me decide how I should get started. After I knew what I wanted to learn I started learning on Sololearn, which is a great free app for beginners. I also spent a lot of time on w3schools.com and css-tricks.com. While I learned a lot about HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap and W3CSS I still felt the need for some extra guidance, especially about Javascript, so I decided to enroll to online courses at Academind.com. Their courses helped me a lot to get a better understanding on the whole picture, on some core features, and on how to write clean code.

I have a quite exact career plan and I want to see myself as a senior web developer as soon as my skills and achievements make it possible. My mid-term goal is to become a full stack developer, and since I am always keen to know more, later on I would like to start learning other programming languages as well. Of course my plan is not written in stone and I am absolutely flexible and open-minded, so if I see a good chance during my career that can alter my plans I will definitely go for it. Currently I am looking for a company that -in spite of the lack of my experience- would give me a chance to grow and help me become a great web developer.

Please have a look at my cv and certificates, and, to show off my Javascript skills, you will find a quiz at the bottom of the site. I hope I have got your attention and I will be able to prove my knowledge in person as well.
